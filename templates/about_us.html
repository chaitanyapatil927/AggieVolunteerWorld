About Us

Aggie Volunteer World is brought to you by Aggie Altruists

Our Mission

The Aggie Altruists team shares multicultural backgrounds but is unified through core values and the Aggie Spirit. We believe that to help the world, we must start by helping our local communities.

Our Team

Aggie Altruists are all former students who got together to give back to Aggieland

Alexander

Platform Manager

Alexander is responsible for overseeing the day-to-day operations of the volunteering platform, ensuring it is functioning properly and meeting the needs of its users. He is also responsible for managing the platform's budget, setting goals and targets for the platform's growth, and tracking key performance metrics.

Chaitanya

Volunteer Manager

Chaitanya is responsible for managing the pool of volunteers registered on the platform. He works to identify volunteer opportunities that align with the skills and interests of registered users, as well as promoting the platform to encourage more Aggies to sign up and volunteer.

Neel

Nonprofit Liaison

Neel is responsible for building and maintaining relationships with nonprofit organizations that partner with the platform. He works to identify new partners, communicate with existing partners to understand their needs and requirements, and troubleshoot any issues that arise.

Padmaja

Content Manager

Padmaja is responsible for developing and executing a comprehensive content strategy that promotes the organization's mission and values. This involves creating engaging and informative content across various digital channels and measuring the effectiveness of the strategy.

Vignesh

Marketing and Communications Specialist

Vignesh develops and implements marketing plans to promote the organization's mission. He creates engaging content, manages social media accounts, and coordinates with the team to plan events and outreach initiatives.

Why You Can Trust Us

At Aggie Volunteer World, we understand the value of finding the right opportunities when it comes to volunteer work. That's why we're committed to providing a trustworthy platform for connecting volunteers with opportunities that match their interests and skills.
